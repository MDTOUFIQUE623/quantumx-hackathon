In plain English: Step 1 is like showing ID at the polling station and getting a stamp on your voter slip. Step 2 is putting your choice in a locked box only officials can open later. Step 3 is keeping your tracking number to prove your ballot was received.

Demo only: Your voting keys stay in this browser tab until you close it. The registration desk checks a sample voter list (not real Aadhaar). Use a row below or see the judge demo guide.
